Roles & Responsibilities

Use this guide to assign roles consistently and keep access aligned with responsibilities. Optional roles such as Supplier and Customer require dedicated portals.

Admin / Owner (Superuser)

Full control of the system and security.

  • Manage users, roles, and permissions
  • Configure POS, AI assistant, and notifications
  • Access all reports, analytics, and audit logs
  • Approve purchase orders and financial decisions
  • Oversee multi-branch operations and AI alerts

Store / Branch Manager

Operational leader for a branch.

  • Oversee inventory levels and transfers
  • Approve purchase orders
  • Monitor daily sales and staff performance
  • Receive AI recommendations and alerts
  • Generate branch reports

Store Staff / Cashier

Day-to-day operations and sales.

  • Scan products and process sales
  • Receive and stock inventory
  • Report damaged or expired items
  • Send and receive internal messages
  • View notifications relevant to duties

Warehouse Staff / Storekeeper

Inventory handling and stock control.

  • Receive incoming shipments
  • Organize warehouse and track locations
  • Perform stock-taking and cycle counts
  • Update inventory records
  • Print labels and barcodes

Delivery Rider

Handles last-mile delivery for door orders.

  • See available delivery orders
  • Accept and update delivery status
  • Confirm delivery with OTP
  • Communicate with branch team

Supplier / Vendor (Optional)

External portal role for purchase order visibility.

  • Receive purchase orders
  • Confirm deliveries and ETA
  • Communicate with procurement team

Customer (Optional)

Online ordering and tracking role.

  • Place orders online
  • Track delivery status
  • Confirm delivery or pickup

System Administrator / IT Support

Technical maintenance and infrastructure.

  • Maintain servers, database, and backups
  • Troubleshoot system issues
  • Deploy updates and integrations
  • Secure environment and access controls

AI Assistant (Virtual Role)

System intelligence and alerts.

  • Monitor inventory and POS in real time
  • Generate predictive alerts and recommendations
  • Answer staff queries and procedures
  • Suggest optimal ordering and pricing
