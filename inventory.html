Problem Focus and Goal

The system tackles inefficient inventory management caused by manual records and disconnected spreadsheets. The goal is to keep purchasing, storage, and sales aligned in one connected workflow.

Key Issues Being Solved

Slow tracking of stock movement Inaccurate stock records Stockouts or overstocking Poor inter-department communication

Company Management — uses reports to make financial and operational decisions.
